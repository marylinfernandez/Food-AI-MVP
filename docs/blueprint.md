# **App Name**: PantryPal AI

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Ingredient Scan: Utilize multimodal AI (Gemini API) to analyze photos or videos of the fridge and pantry, accurately identifying and quantifying available food items. This tool automatically populates the digital pantry.
- Personalized Profile Builder: An AI-driven voice interface to guide the user through setting up their family profile, including number of residents, ages, dietary restrictions, allergies, taste preferences, and available cooking appliances, ensuring minimal friction.
- Contextual Recipe Suggestions: Dynamically generate personalized recipe recommendations based on identified ingredients, stored user/family profiles, specified complexity levels (prioritizing practicality), and available cooking time, aimed at eliminating decision fatigue.
- Voice-Enabled Pantry Management: Allow users to update their pantry inventory (e.g., mark an item as consumed, confirm ingredient recognition accuracy) through simple, natural voice commands, integrated with proactive audio interactions.
- Smart Shopping Integration: Analyze photos of grocery receipts or shopping cart contents using AI to automatically detect and add newly purchased ingredients to the digital pantry, maintaining an up-to-date inventory without manual entry.
- Customizable Audio Interface: Provide options for users to select preferred voice, accent, and language (e.g., Spanish Latin, English) for all audio responses and proactive communications from the AI assistant.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme is used to evoke clarity, freshness, and an 'effortless' experience.
- Primary color: A sophisticated, calming blue (#296BCC), chosen for its connotations of intelligence and tranquility, promoting an atmosphere of effortless problem-solving.
- Background color: An ultra-light, subtle grey-blue (#EEF1F5) that offers a clean, expansive canvas, ensuring text and interactive elements stand out clearly without causing eye strain.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet serene sky blue (#4DD6F7), strategically applied to highlight key actions and reinforce the feeling of a 'friction-zero' and intuitive interface.
- Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headlines and body text. Its clean, modern, and highly legible design ensures optimal readability, minimizing cognitive effort and enhancing the seamless user experience.
- Use minimalist, clean-lined icons. These will represent food categories, household items, and app functionalities in a straightforward manner, contributing to the 'friction-zero' interface.
- A spacious and minimalist layout, prioritizing clear hierarchy and ample white space. This design approach reduces visual clutter, focusing user attention on one key task or piece of information at a time to reduce cognitive load.
- Subtle, fluid animations and transitions for navigation and content loading. These movements will be purposeful, providing gentle feedback to user actions without drawing excessive attention, reinforcing the seamless and 'essential' assistant feel.
